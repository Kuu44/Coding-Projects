The sky runner!

Outrun the incoming doom of red as the platforms disappear behind you!



-----------------------------
Controls:
W or UP arrow - Move Forward
S or DOWN arrow - Move Backwards
A and D or RIGHT and LEFT arrows - Turn right or left
Left mouse button + drag - look around (CAN'T BE USED TO TURN THE CHARACTER ITSELF)

SPACE or RIGHT mouse button - JUMP

SHIFT or '0' or 'f' - HIGH SPEED MODE! (MOVING AND JUMPING ENHANCED WHILE THIS IS PRESSED)

M - TOGGLE EASY/HARD MODE

R - RESTART AFTER GAME OVER
-------------------------------
